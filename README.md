#### How I work

Take vague problems and turn them into shipped products by end of day. Work across product, engineering, and architecture simultaneously. Agentic engineering with multiple agents. Every week the tools get better, every week the ambition scales.

I build things on nights and weekends not because I have to, but because I think software should make people's lives better and most of the important problems haven't been touched yet. 7,000+ rare diseases have no dedicated AI tools. Amateur sports have no AI officiating. Small research teams can't afford what pharma companies have.

Those are the gaps. That's where I build.
